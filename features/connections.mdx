<Tip>
Connections determine what OpenSeller can see, sync, and reason about. The more context your agent has, the better its recommendations.
</Tip>

The **Connections** section is where you review available integrations across marketplaces, messaging, analytics, productivity, payments, shipping, and research tools.

## What Connections do

Connections unlock real context for the agent:

- **Marketplace connections** help with listings, orders, and sales context
- **Messaging connections** let your agent reach you where you already work
- **Analytics connections** improve profit and reporting workflows
- **Research connections** improve trend discovery and market analysis

## Key integrations

### Etsy

Etsy is the most important marketplace connection for OpenSeller.

- Import Etsy CSV data
- Sync listings, orders, and reviews
- Track sales performance and trends
- Monitor competitor listings

### Telegram

Telegram acts as your mobile command center:

- Chat with your agent on the go
- Receive daily briefs and alerts
- Ask quick business questions
- Stay connected without opening the dashboard

### Profit View

Profit View is the connected analytics layer for true-profit work:

- True profit per order
- COGS and fee visibility
- Monthly P&L reporting
- Break-even analysis

### Brave Search & OpenRouter

These power the research and AI side of the product:

- **Brave Search** — trend and competitor research
- **OpenRouter** — model routing for the agent

## Connection statuses

You'll see these statuses in the connections catalog:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `Connected` | Active and syncing data |
| `Available` | Ready to set up |
| `Coming Soon` | On the roadmap |

## Recommended setup order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your messaging channel">
    This is how your agent reaches you. Start here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your primary data source">
    Usually Etsy. This gives the agent real shop context to work with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add analytics or reporting tools">
    Profit View, reporting integrations — whatever helps you track money.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add research tools as needed">
    Brave Search, OpenRouter — only when you're ready for trend discovery and deeper analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Connections aren't just badges — they're how OpenSeller gets better context for your actual decisions. If a workflow feels shallow, check whether the right data source is connected first.
</Warning>
